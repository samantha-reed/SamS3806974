Learning style:

What do the results of these tests mean for you?

According to this assessment, Im an Auditory learner. This result suggests I learn best hearing a lecture rather than reading a book or writing lines. I feel conflicted about this result as I often will become overwhelmed by surrounding or background noises, thus not be able to take in the information during lectures. However, I also success passed competences by listening to lectures alone.

How do you think these results may influence your behaviour in a team?

I feel this could affect my ability in a team as a lot of IT communication is now based around utilising email or an instant messenger. This could affect how efficiently I could be trained as I wouldnt learn as well if I was given an instructional book to read. How information is presented would directly impact on my capability to utilise such information.

How should you take this into account when forming a team?

An auditory learner is more easily distracted in a high traffic area noise so would need to be moved away from that environment for a productive outcome. However, as audio input is vital, so access to headphones for music would support positive outcomes.
